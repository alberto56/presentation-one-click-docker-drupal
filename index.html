Ideal local development?

Ideal local development principles

  • 1. No dependencies
  • 2. Not platform-specific
  • 3. No GUI
  • 4. Not provider-specific
  • 5. Lightning fast
  • 6. Codebase self-contained
  • 7. One-click
  • 8. Resembles production

Ideal local development principle #1

No dependencies

(well... maybe Docker. But nothing else.)

Ideal local development principle #2

Not platform-specific

Deploy on Mac, Windows, or a Cloud VM

Ideal local development principle #3

No GUI

A strictly command-line approach is scriptable and extensible.

Ideal local development principle #4

Not provider-specific

Not tied to your production host
(Kalabox, Acquia Dev Desktop...)

Ideal local development principle #5

Lightning fast

Kind of the opposite of Vagrant.

Ideal local development principle #6

Codebase self-contained

(databases are not version controlled).

Ideal local development principle #7

One-click (or one command)

Ideal local development principle #8

Resembles production

Any difference between environments
will eventually lead to a failure.
